Researcher + Artist

I'm interested in understanding music, technology, society, and how they shape each other. In my work as a musician and researcher I bring together a macro-level analysis of institutions and structures that play a part in how culture is produced, circulated, and consumed, with a micro-level view that takes into account the everyday lives of cultural producers, music fans, and workers in the creative industries. I'm a research fellow at the School of Media and Communications, University of Leeds, and an associate researcher in the project ‘Practice-led PhD Programme’ at the Leeds Institute for Teaching Excellence.
